Project Goals

Mr Blackie came to me asking for a book holder for his classroom and for recipes. I asked him what colour would suit and he said white. White is a neutral colour that works well in a kitchen.

I changed the filament in the 3D printer to white and set it to work. It took about 4 hours. I like to check it as its printing because sometimes the head clogs up and its a spagetti like mess. This is fixed by cleaning the head with a bamboo lubricant oil.

I had no problems for this print and handed it to Mr Blacke the next day. He was happy. We did a deal on the price because he has helped me by hosting my 3D printer in his class.
